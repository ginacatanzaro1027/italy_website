History of Italy

Italy has a rich history that dates back thousands of years, from the Roman Empire to the Renaissance. The country has contributed greatly to art, science, and culture. Italy became a unified nation in 1861 after centuries of independent city-states.

Famous People from Italy

  • Leonardo da Vinci - Artist & Inventor
  • Galileo Galilei - Scientist
  • Michelangelo - Sculptor & Painter
  • Christopher Columbus - Explorer
  • Enrico Fermi - Physicist
